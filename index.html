About Panos

Story

My mom and dad met working at Seagalley, and they soon fell in love. Though my mother moved to Alaska, they reunited and wed in Seattle. Everything was looking up. Then I was born. A rambunctious demon child, my unholy nature caused a pitchfork wielding mob to chase my family to Boise. There I grew and learned to hide my sinister nature, until I was 12, at which point we moved back to Seattle. Middle and High School passed uneventfully, followed by some college and food service stuff; now I am here.

Family

I have a father and a mother, one half brother and one full younger brother. I am fortunate to have a lot of family live nearby: my paternal grandparents, my aunt and uncle, my greataunt, and many cousins.
